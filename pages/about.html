About

Hello and thank you for stopping by! 👋

I'm Andrew, a London-based design and strategy expert that has spent the last 10+ years designing digital tools and services for organisations in the financial services, life sciences, education, housing, and transportation industries.

Currently, I'm a service designer with the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA). I provide end-to-end research and design expertise to project teams responsible for delivering internal case management and intelligence services. I also lead on the development and governance of the FCA's design system and interaction design toolkit.

Prior to joining the FCA, I was a lead user experience designer with the Money and Pensions Service and designed digital tools and services for MoneyHelper. I also worked with Open Targets as a product design & strategy lead and supported the development of open-source digital tools for early stage drug discovery. I have also worked in design and strategy roles for other organisations in Canada, the United States, and the United Kingdom. Additionally, I have delivered lectures and design workshops in the UK, Switzerland, Spain, and Brazil, and have also authored content for an online design course.

To learn more about me and my approach to design, check out my latest projects, my archived portfolio, and my blog.
