About

Hello! My name is Andrew Hercules and I am a Cambridge-based user experience (UX) researcher and designer and digital strategist. And yes, in case you are wondering, my last name really is Hercules!

Currently, I am the UX lead for Open Targets, a unique public-private partnership that integrates genetics and genomics data to help scientists identify and prioritise potential therapeutic drug targets. Prior to joining the Open Targets project, I worked in UX- and design-related roles for various Canadian and European organisations. I have also worked as a designer/developer specialising in research and data repositories, a communications coordinator for the University of Toronto, a writer/producer/presenter for Radio Netherlands Worldwide, a freelance digital strategist, an assistant caseworker, and a youth employment specialist.
